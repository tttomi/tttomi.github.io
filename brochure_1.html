Our mission is to solve the challenges related to climate change mitigation by creating new sustainable solutions for energy technology

  • Together with the customers
  • Passion for innovations in the field of renewable energy

Our vision is to be the leading partner in creating sustainable solutions for energy in transition

  • In co-operation with European leading research institutes and universities
  • Bring together different customers and partners for ambitious innovation iniatives

Our competence areas are:

  • Feedstock supply chain management
  • Optimisation of multifuel combustion processes
  • Development of efficient Renewable Energy Source (RES) hybrid solutions, e.g. solar-biomass hybrid systems
  • Carbon capture and utilisation pathways
  • Dynamic analysis and optimisation
    of RES processes
  • Tools for 100% RES implementation
