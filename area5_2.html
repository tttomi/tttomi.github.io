Properties of indigenous fuels
in Finland Study To menu

VTT published a summary of the key properties of wood, peat, coal, agrobiomass, sludge, natural gas, biogas as well as mineral and bio oils.

The summary can be particularly utilised by power plants, fuel producers, equipment manufacturers and educational institutes. For the indigenous fuels in Finland, the production technology and potential are also partially covered.

The publication describes the most important quality analysis methods and the applied standards. The effects of the fuel properties on the combustion and gasification are also introduced.
