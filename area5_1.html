VTT has vast experience in bioenergy research in different
parts of the world. In Europe, promising results from
projects executed in Poland, the Czech Republic, France
and Ukraine have been achieved. These feasibility study
type projects have dealt with local forest energy potential,
optimized procurement technology and with the economic
issues of biomass heat and power generation.
VTT has experts in all areas of
bioenergy - from technological
questions to business concepts and
policies and to economic questions.

VTT actively looks for opportunities for launching new projects in European locations.
