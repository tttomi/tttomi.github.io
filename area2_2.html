Corrosion and fouling Concept
Corrosion and fouling of the boiler surfaces result in unwanted load restrictions on the power production. The phenomena can be researched with demonstrative pilot-scale tests, which enable:

  1. Following the corrosion and fouling of the boiler surfaces
    • Effect of the changes in the fuel blend
    • Effect of the changes in the combustion process

  2. Acting accordingly
    • Selection of the materials used in the boiler surfaces
    • Selection of the used fuel blend
    • More accurate prediction and planning of shutdowns in advance
    • Optimising soot-blowing frequency

As a result, new challenging fuel blends (e.g. biomass and wastes) can be safely utilised, the boiler availability can be increased (less unwanted shutdowns and maintenance)
